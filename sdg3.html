What is SDG 3?

SDG Goal 3 – Good Health & Well-Being focuses on ensuring that everyone lives a healthy, safe, and productive life. It promotes access to quality healthcare, awareness, mental health support, and disease prevention to build stronger and healthier communities.

About Our Initiative – Wellness Way

Wellness Way is a community-centered program dedicated to spreading awareness about healthy living, nutrition, mental health, fitness, and preventive care. We aim to educate people about healthier habits and support them in building a balanced lifestyle.

Our Focus Areas

  • Preventive Healthcare – Vaccination, screenings, hygiene awareness
  • Mental Wellness – Stress relief, emotional support, counseling awareness
  • Nutrition Education – Balanced diet guidance, healthy eating habits
  • Physical Fitness – Yoga, exercise, sports activities
  • Community Health Camps – Free checkups and medical support
